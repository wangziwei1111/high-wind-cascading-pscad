# Model Deviations

- PNNL three-IBR uses WECC-approved generic IBR models, not the thesis DFIG wind farm model.
- PSCAD official Type-3 WTG is a candidate DFIG module, not automatically equivalent to the thesis WECC second-generation Type-3 configuration.
- Target wind buses 33, 35, 38 come from the thesis; PNNL IBR locations must be verified manually and may not match.
- MATLAB protection settings are parameterized and include documented assumptions where exact thesis values were not recoverable from text extraction.
